Return of Colorful Mod Pack

Return of Colorful Mod Pack is a modpack I created back in 2016 to play modded Minecraft with friends. It featured various mods such as RedPower, Twilight Forest, Tropicraft, Iron Furnaces, and much more! This modpack has been updated to Minecraft 1.10.2 to feature the latest of those classic mods! Enjoy!
